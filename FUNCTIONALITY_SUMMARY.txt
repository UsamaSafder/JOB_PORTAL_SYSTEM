## Ready to Use

The admin panel is **production-ready** and can be used immediately to manage:
- Candidates (view, filter, toggle status, delete)
- Companies (view, filter, verify, toggle status, delete)
- Jobs (view, filter, toggle status, delete)
- System activities (view, search, filter, export)

All changes are persisted to the database and logged for audit purposes.
